Dr. Jacob R. Mittleider

Food For Everyone Foundation

Dr. Mittleider had run a successful nursery business for 20 years, and then began traveling the world, educating the local population in 27 countries about how to grow food efficiently.

  • Jacob Mittlider made a significant contribution to the theory and practice of mineral nutrition in vegetable crops.

  • Jacob Mittlider formulated his method of growing plants when he ran the Vegetable Growing for Small Family Gardens training program in 27 different countries.

  • Mittlider was a commercial seedling producer in a nursery in Southern California. Meatlider holds over 11 US patents.

  • Jacob Mittlider developed commercial methods for growing, packaging, and airborne live plants for sale.

  • Jacob Mittlider has over 17 years of experience in floriculture. He bred 5 varieties of carnations, for which he received copyright certificates.

  • Jacob Mittlider has developed a unique greenhouse design called Dr. Mittlider's Greenhouse Mittlider's Greenhouse or Mittlider's Greenhouse.
